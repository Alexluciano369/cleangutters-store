1. Platform Strategy Overview

Your growth stacks across platforms if you do it right. The play: create once, distribute everywhere, optimize per platform.

The Cross-Platform Funnel

Top of Funnel: TikTok + Reels — reach new audiences. Short-form video. Entertain or educate in under 60 seconds.

Middle of Funnel: X + LinkedIn — build authority. Thread-style education. Show your expertise. Turn viewers into followers.

Bottom of Funnel: Email list + newsletter — own the relationship. Convert followers into customers.

2. X (Twitter) Growth Playbook

The Algorithm in 2025-2026

X rewards: replies (especially to large accounts), long-form threads, and media. It penalizes: external links (put them in the second tweet or reply), overuse of hashtags, and engagement bait phrasing.

Daily X Routine (1 hour/day)

  1. Morning (15 min): Reply to 5-10 accounts with 10K+ followers. Add value — don't just say "great thread." Add a counterpoint, a related experience, or additional data.
  2. Midday (15 min): Post 1 thread or long-form post. Your best content goes here.
  3. Afternoon (15 min): Post 2-3 shorter posts — observations, questions, quick takes.
  4. Evening (15 min): Engage with your replies. Respond to every comment for the first hour after posting.
Growth Hack: Find 5 accounts with 50K-200K followers in your niche. Turn on notifications. Be the first valuable reply on their posts every day for 30 days. This alone can net you 500-2,000 followers/month.

Thread Structure That Works

  1. Tweet 1 (Hook): Counterintuitive statement or bold claim. End with "Here's why:"
  2. Tweet 2: The problem or context. Why should they care?
  3. Tweets 3-5: The meat. One key point per tweet. Self-contained.
  4. Tweet 6: Bonus insight or counter-argument. Show depth.
  5. Tweet 7: Summary + CTA. "Follow @you for more on [topic]."

3. LinkedIn Growth Playbook

The LinkedIn Algorithm

LinkedIn's algorithm shows your post to a small sample of your network first. If engagement is high (comments > likes), it expands. The "golden hour" (first 60 minutes) determines everything.

Content Mix for LinkedIn

  1. Story-Lesson Posts (40%) — Personal story + business lesson. These get the most comments.
  2. Carousels/PDFs (25%) — Slide-style documents. LinkedIn pushes these hard in feed.
  3. Contrarian Takes (20%) — Disagree with common advice. Be respectful but firm.
  4. Curated Insights (15%) — "5 lessons from [book/experience/person]" style.
Key Insight: LinkedIn's "creator mode" + consistent posting = 5-10x reach increase within 60 days. Turn on creator mode, pick 5 hashtags, and post daily for 2 months.

4. Instagram Growth Playbook

Instagram is Reels-first now. Static posts have 70% less reach than Reels. Your strategy: Reels for reach, Carousels for saves, Stories for connection.

Reel Formula That Works

  1. Hook (0-1.5s): Visual + text hook simultaneously. Movement in the first frame.
  2. Value (1.5-20s): 3 quick tips. One per "chapter." Text on screen for each.
  3. CTA (last 3s): "Follow for more [niche] tips" or "Comment [word] for the template"

Hashtag strategy: 3-5 specific hashtags (under 500K posts) + 1-2 broad ones. Don't use 30 — it looks spammy and doesn't help.

5. TikTok Growth Playbook

TikTok is the most meritocratic platform. Quality content wins regardless of follower count. The algorithm tests your video on 200-500 people. High watch time + completion rate = it gets pushed further.

TikTok Video Structure

  1. 0-1s Pattern Interrupt: Something that makes them stop scrolling. Quick movement, surprising statement, or visual contrast.
  2. 1-3s Hook Statement: "Most people don't know that..." / "Stop doing X if you want Y..."
  3. 3-45s Value Delivery: Fast-paced. Jump cuts every 3-5 seconds. Keep energy high.
  4. Last 3s CTA: Loop-friendly ending. "Follow for part 2" creates bookmark behavior.

8. Engagement Automation

DM Outreach Templates

After they follow you: "Hey [name] — saw you're into [topic]. What are you working on right now in that space?"

After they engage with your content: "Thanks for the thoughtful comment on my [post]. Curious — what's your take on [related question]?"

Collaboration ask: "Love your content on [topic]. I'm putting together a [thread/post/space] about [specific angle] — would you be open to contributing a quote or insight?"
