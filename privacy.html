Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

What we collect

When you buy a product or request a free sample, we collect your email address and, for purchases, the billing details you provide to our payment processor. We use Stripe for payments — your card information is handled by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

How we use it

We use your email to deliver your purchase, send the materials you requested, and occasionally share relevant tips or new products. Every email includes an unsubscribe link.

Who we share it with

We share data only with the services that make the store work: Stripe (payments) and our email provider (delivery). We never sell your data to third parties.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time, and you can request deletion of your data by emailing alex@thetradesguy.com. We honor all such requests promptly.

Cookies & analytics

We use lightweight, privacy-respecting analytics to understand which pages perform. We do not run invasive third-party ad trackers.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email alex@thetradesguy.com.
